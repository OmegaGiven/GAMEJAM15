# Fear from the Shadows

**By OMEGA and friends — Pirate Software Game Jam 15 (2024)**

## Summary

In a marsh surrounded by darkness, armed with nothing but an axe and torch, you must keep your campfire burning as shadows close in. Survive long enough to venture into the dark and fight the source sending waves at you. Grab a second controller for couch co-op chaos.

## Genre

RTS / Couch Co-op / Survival

## Target Audience

People who want a quick 15–30 minute round of chaotic survival with a friend.

## Platform

- PC
- Switch *(eventually)*
- Xbox
- PlayStation *(eventually)*

## Core Loop

1. Chop trees to gather wood
2. Feed the campfire to keep it lit — fire = health
3. Build light objects (spirit totems) to defend the fire from shadow enemies
4. Survive waves long enough to go fight the boss in the dark
5. Fire goes out → you lose

## Theme

**Shadows and Alchemy** — scary things live in the dark; use the alchemy of fire to fend them off.

## Primary Mechanics

| Mechanic | Description |
|---|---|
| Player lighting | Light radius tied to resource count |
| Wood gathering | Attack trees to get firewood |
| Feed the fire | Add wood to campfire to sustain it |
| Boss fight | Venture into the dark to fight the source of the waves |

## Secondary Mechanics

| Mechanic | Description |
|---|---|
| Couch co-op | 2-player local split-screen |
| PvP Arcade mode | Both players survive but can attack each other's campfire — RTS-style |

## Art Direction

Spooky woods. Player should always feel afraid of what they can't see. Enemies appear from the darkness unexpectedly. **Light = safety.**

## Audio

- Music: eerie ambient noise, not traditional music
- SFX: subtle random sounds to spook the player

## UI

Minimal/no UI to preserve immersion. **Light objects are the health indicator** — no health bars.

## Controls

**Keyboard:** WASD / Arrow keys, mouse for attacking and zoom

**Gamepad:** Joystick + triggers for movement/attack, buttons for building

## Built With

- Godot 4.2 (GDScript)
- Art: Aseprite / GIMP / Paint
- Audio: Ableton
